# **App Name**: Artisan Portfolio

## Core Features:

- Project Gallery: Showcase projects with descriptions, images, and details.
- About Me Section: Introduce yourself with a professional bio and personal details.
- Skills Display: Visually represent your skills with progress bars or icons.
- Contact Form: Enable visitors to contact you directly through an integrated form.
- AI Portfolio Curator Tool: An AI tool that will review your projects and determine an optimal organization and tagging to enhance presentation.
- Dark Mode Toggle: Allows the user to toggle between light and dark mode themes, according to personal preference.

## Style Guidelines:

- Color palette: Primary color: Deep blue (#293462) to evoke professionalism and modernity. This color conveys trustworthiness and expertise, crucial for a portfolio.
- Background color: Dark gray (#333333) to complement the deep blue and provide a sleek, minimalist feel.
- Accent color: Soft gray (#A7A7A7) for subtle highlights and text elements, maintaining a clean aesthetic.
- Font pairing: 'Space Grotesk' (sans-serif) for headlines and 'Inter' (sans-serif) for body text. These choices offer a modern, professional aesthetic with excellent readability.
- Use simple, clean icons for skills and navigation, aligning with the minimalist theme.
- Minimalist layout with a focus on visual hierarchy to showcase the most important projects first.
- Subtle transition effects and animations to enhance user experience without being distracting.
